About

Good day and welcome to my digital portfolio! I am Sharmaine Louisse Reginaldo Villafuerte with the initials SLRV (sounds like eselarvi). I am currently a third year college student from iACADEMY, Makati taking AB Multimedia Arts and Design. As a Multimedia Art Major, my goal is to expand my experience and expertise in the field of digital media through creative works and internship.

This digital portfolio showcases my best works in different forms of multimedia which represents my skills and experience as a person and an art student.
